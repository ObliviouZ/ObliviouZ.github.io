The objectives of PolyQ Database

What is this for?

  This was a work done by a thesis student of biomedical sciences.

  This website will be presented in the thesis presention of said student in order to achieve the master degree in biomedical sciences.

  This website is also a tool for scientists looking for information on polyglutamine diseases, all in one place.

Why was this made?

Information about polyglutamine disorders is scattered through the internet, so one single website regarding the most important information on every polyglutamine disease would surely be helpful for the science community.

What makes this database different from others?

Until the date of the creation of this database, there is no other polyglutamine database that includes all general information about every polyglutamine disease, some try to focus only on the genetic side of the disease, others on the therapheutic side of it. With the information found on the web, the most searched information on polyglutamine disorders is consolidated within this website.
